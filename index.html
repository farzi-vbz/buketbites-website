About BucketBites

BucketBites started with a simple mission: to serve the crispiest, most flavorful fried chicken in Kerala using our secret recipe sauce.

What began as a small family restaurant has now grown to 7 locations across Kerala, each maintaining the same quality and taste that made us famous.

We take pride in using fresh ingredients and preparing each dish with care, ensuring every bite is as delicious as the first.
